╰┈➤ Relations with Other Empires

Like many other successful civilizations, Kyivan Rus' had political relationships with other nations. Some of these nations were also very popular and were quite historically influential, such as the Byzantine Empire. As they held a lot of power during the time, many tribes and ethnic groups had relationships with Rus', with some being positive and negative. Rus' interacted with many tribes, as the country itself was formed from it and ruled by Vikings, being multiethnic. Through these relations, the multicultural world of Kyivan Rus' can be seen through their culture, trade, government, and religion.

As we reach the end of our story, let us all look back at how the connections and goals of many can lead to the start of an empire. Russia is now the largest and one of the most politically and economically influential countries in the world. With proper leadership, development, and connections, power can really be achieved.
